What is the eBay Sidebar for Firefox?

The eBay Sidebar is an add-on for the Mozilla Firefox browser that allows you to keep track of your eBay buying and selling activities. It gives you an at-a-glance view of My eBay and customisable in-browser alerts about eBay events, like when you’re outbid or successfully purchase or sell an item.
